Plainnote

Plain markdown notes,
no clutter.

Plainnote is a minimal notes app for Mac - a clean space to write, nothing extra. Every note is a plain markdown file on your disk.

Download for Mac

  • Your files, on your disk

    Notes live in a folder you choose. Move them, back them up, sync them however you like.

  • Plain .md, no database

    Every note is an ordinary markdown file. Readable in any editor, today and in twenty years.

  • No account, no telemetry

    Nothing to sign up for. Plainnote doesn't phone home, and it works entirely offline.

  • Open source

    AGPL-3.0 licensed, all of it on GitHub. You can read every line of the app that holds your notes.
